About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Mathematics at the University of California San Diego, working in number theory and representation theory under the guidance of Prof. Claus Sorensen. My research focuses on stratifying the derived category of mod-p representations of p-adic lie groups.

I am also interested in applications of mathematics in different fields, with graduate coursework in probabilistic machine learning and mathematical finance.

I have taught or served as a teaching assistant for various undergraduate courses including Calculus, Linear Algebra, Probability, and Cryptography. I am passionate about mathematical pedagogy, having directly worked with high school students and having been involved in training high school educators.
